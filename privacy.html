SharpSense privacy policy

Effective 12 June 2026

SharpSense is a daily thinking puzzle for product managers. The short version: there are no accounts, we do not know who you are, and we would like to keep it that way.

What we collect

Nothing personal. SharpSense has no sign-up, no login, and no fields where you type anything. We never collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, location, or anything that identifies you.

Your progress

Your levels, streaks and lessons are stored on your device only. Deleting the app deletes them.

Anonymous usage analytics

To understand which parts of the game work, the app records anonymous events such as "a level was completed" or "a warm-up was skipped", tagged with a random identifier that is not linked to you. These events never contain question text or anything you did outside the app. They are processed by PostHog on servers in the United States, and are used for nothing except improving the game. They are not linked to your identity and are not used to track you across other apps or websites.

Notifications

If you choose to set a daily reminder, it is scheduled on your device. We do not send remote notifications.

No ads, no sale of data

SharpSense shows no advertising, contains no third-party trackers, and sells no data, because it has none to sell.

Children

SharpSense collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
